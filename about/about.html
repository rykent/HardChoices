When was it made?

It was made in the March, 2019 intersession during the Experimental Game Design class at Design Tech High School with the Oracle Education Foundation.
The game was chosen by the Oracle Education to go to Oracle OpenWorld and be presented at the Oracle Education Foundation booth.

What will happen with the game next?

We plan to continue to develop the game and expand the reach of it. Our goal is to provide lots of people with more knowledge on this issue through this game.
